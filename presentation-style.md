# CTA Presentation Style Guide

## Colors (ServiceNow Brand)
- **Primary Dark**: #0B2D4E (dark navy — backgrounds, headers)
- **Primary Green**: #62D84E (ServiceNow green — accents, highlights, icons)
- **White**: #FFFFFF (text on dark backgrounds, slide backgrounds)
- **Light Gray**: #F4F4F4 (subtle section backgrounds)
- **Mid Gray**: #6B7280 (secondary text, labels)
- **Accent Light Blue**: #E8F4FD (light highlight boxes)

## Typography
- **Title font**: Calibri Bold, 36-40pt, White on dark / Dark navy on white
- **Subtitle / Section header**: Calibri Bold, 24-28pt
- **Body text**: Calibri, 18pt
- **Notes / Labels**: Calibri, 14pt, Mid Gray
- **No serif fonts**

## Layout Principles
- Clean white or dark navy backgrounds — never busy
- Max. 5 bullet points per slide
- Every slide has a clear single message (the "so what")
- Use icons or simple visuals instead of clipart
- Tables: dark header row (#0B2D4E white text), alternating rows (#F4F4F4 / white)
- Consistent left-aligned title bar with green accent line underneath

## Slide Structure
- **Title area**: dark navy bar across top, white title text, green accent line
- **Content area**: white background, dark text
- **Footer**: slide number bottom right, subtle gray

## Dos and Don'ts
- Do: use plenty of white space
- Do: one idea per slide
- Do: visual hierarchy (title > heading > body)
- Don't: walls of text
- Don't: more than 2 font sizes on one slide
- Don't: use red/green for compliance status without colorblind-safe alternative labels
