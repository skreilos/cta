Role of the Technical Architect

The role of the Technical Architect

The Technical Architect is responsible for analyzing and translating business information and technical requirements into architectural blueprints, proposing solutions to achieve complex business objectives. The position provides guidance on, assists in establishing, and participates in technical governance processes. The role collaborates on the design and implementation of a platform operating model that improves likelihood of successful go-lives and end-user adoption. Technical Architects provide technical evaluation of strategic and operational demands against the ServiceNow platform architecture, platform capabilities, and leading practices.

In addition, the Technical Architect can also provide a technical gateway between the initial designs agreed in the sales conversations through to the delivery cycles with the project managers, business analysts, solution architects, and developers.

 

The Certified Technical Architect is acknowledged by implementers and developers as a source of experience and thought leadership, as well as being a key contributor in digital transformation.

The Certified Technical Architect key functions include:

  • Translating customer requirements or outcomes into architectural designs
  • Developing solutions that comply with common application designs, leading practices, and meet business requirements
  • Contributing to the structuring and implementation of solutions working directly with senior leaders on challenging projects
  • Providing guidance on, assisting in establishing, and participating in technical governance processes
  • Leveraging their extensive platform experience to assist customers to develop their journey further, reducing risk and minimizing technical debt

ServiceNow certification track

The CTA certification is part of the ServiceNow Expert Programs. The Certified Technical Architect is a critical role in the ServiceNow ecosystem.

 

Program objectives

Successfully implementing and managing the ServiceNow platform and solutions requires more than technical product expertise: there is a need for professionals that can combine technical and professional skills to help customers successfully implement.

To help deliver customers’ business objectives and transformation visions, ServiceNow platform architects needed to learn and develop these skills:

  • Technical
  • Service management
  • Operational skills
  • “Soft” skills, including communication, collaboration, negotiation, and influencing others

 

What the program offers

The ServiceNow CTA certification demonstrates your knowledge, skills, and ability to:  

  • Analyze and translate business information and technical requirements into an architectural blueprint, proposing solutions to achieve complex business objectives 
  • Guide on, assist in establishing, and participate in technical governance processes
  • Collaborate on the design and implementation of a platform operating model that improves the likelihood of successful go-lives and end-user adoption
  • Provide technical evaluation of strategic and operational demands against ServiceNow platform architecture, platform capabilities, and leading practices

A goal of the program is to increase the number of CTAs who can architect ServiceNow solutions that meet their customer's current and future business outcomes.
