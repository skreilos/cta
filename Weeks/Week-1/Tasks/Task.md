### Current and To-Be Architecture: Group Project

## About this Project

**Assignment Due Date:** March 26, 2026 8:00 AM
**Review Due Date:** March 27, 2026 12:00 AM

In this project, groups will step into the role of Certified Technical Architects (CTA) to conduct an architecture assessment for GlobalPort Logistics and create a to-be architecture. Your group will use a capability map to evaluate the current state of the organization's ServiceNow implementation, identify, and highlight the hotspots (i.e., areas of underutilization or those that need improvement and focus). The goal is to align these insights with organizational priorities and deliver a future architecture that strengthens current adoption while enabling growth, scalability, and long-term effectiveness.

During this project, you will:
- Assess the current ServiceNow architecture to identify gaps, risks, and opportunities for improvement
- Build a capability map that highlights process strengths, weaknesses, and areas for optimization
- Design a proposed to-be architecture that addresses hotspots and aligns with business priorities
