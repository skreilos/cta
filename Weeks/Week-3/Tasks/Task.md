# Week 3 - Tasks & Activities

## Technical Governance: Group Project

### Deadlines
- **Assignment Due**: April 16, 2026 08:00 AM
- **Peer Review Due**: April 17, 2026 12:00 AM (midnight)

### Context
Groups take on the role of Certified Technical Architects (CTAs) to design a technical governance board structure for a customer.

### Deliverables
- [ ] Design the technical governance board structure:
  - [ ] Define the board's purpose
  - [ ] Identify members and roles
  - [ ] Outline scope and guidelines
  - [ ] Establish governance processes
  - [ ] Propose success measures to evaluate board effectiveness
- [ ] Submit team deliverables (one team member uploads all files)
- [ ] Complete peer review of another group's project (−10% penalty if missed)
- [ ] Record individual presentation communicating the group's final deliverable
- [ ] Receive AI-powered feedback on presentation delivery

### Learning Objectives
- Practical insights for guiding customers in setting up and evolving effective ServiceNow governance structures
- Demonstrate how a TGB addresses emerging business needs: informed decisions, stakeholder alignment, risk mitigation
- Develop shared understanding of how governance forums support continuous platform and process improvement
