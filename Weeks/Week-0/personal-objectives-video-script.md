# Personal Objectives – Video Script

Hi, my name is Stephan Kreilos. I work as a ServiceNow Architect at BitHawk in Switzerland, and I've been working with ServiceNow for about seven years now.

So, why the CTA program?

The main reason is that I want to keep learning. I have a lot of experience, and this program helps me learn the foundations and techniques that I can use directly in my daily work.

I also like a good challenge, and the CTA is definitely one.

And then there's the certification. My customers and my employer trust me with important decisions. The CTA is a way to show that this trust is well placed.

So for me it comes down to three things: learning more, taking on a real challenge, and having a certification that reflects what I've built up over the years.

I'm looking forward to it.
