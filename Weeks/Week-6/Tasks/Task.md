## Overview

Groups step into the role of CTAs to analyze UX challenges in ServiceNow and recommend UX-driven solutions for GlobalFin Services. Identify pain points, propose solutions via mockups, and communicate recommendations to stakeholders.

## What the assignment requires

1. Identify employee pain points and connect them to UX challenges
2. Recommend ServiceNow UX solutions through mockups (workspace + portal)
3. Apply content architecture principles and content organization schemes
4. Communicate recommendations tailored to stakeholders, aligned with org goals

## Task 1 — Workspace Mockup (Now Experience Framework)

Address UX challenges faced by IT analysts. The mockup must cover:

**Now Experience elements:** Which elements will be used and how they are configured

**Widgets and components:** Suggest components (lists, dashboards, timeline, related records, notifications) that help IT analysts manage tickets, view SLAs, track due dates, and review user-related tickets from one location

**Content:** Propose a content organization scheme and structure using content architecture principles and content creation leading practices

Reference: Service Operations Workspace — study how it is structured and designed before creating the mockup.

## Task 2 — Service Portal Homepage Mockup

Address UX challenges faced by employees. The mockup must cover:

**Service Portal technology:** Specify the technologies recommended (AngularJS, Bootstrap, JavaScript) for a responsive, customized homepage

**Portal tools:** Identify the tools for configuring and managing the homepage

**Portal widgets:** Recommend standard and custom widgets that address each employee challenge

**Content:** Propose a content organization scheme using content architecture principles and content creation leading practices

### GlobalFin Services — Pain Points Summary

**IT Analyst pain points (Task 1):**
- No central location to track assigned cases and tickets
- No visibility into due dates and SLAs
- Frequent page toggling to access information
- Hard to review all tickets related to a user

**Employee pain points (Task 2):**
- Hard to find service requests
- No intuitive way to track open request status
- Knowledge articles difficult to browse
- No single pane for assigned tasks and submitted tickets
